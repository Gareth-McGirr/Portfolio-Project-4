WELCOME

With arguably the best views in the area, Sizzle and Steak certainly offers food and drink with a view! With tables gazing out to the Atlantic Ocean and beyond, it offers the perfect place to eat, drink and relax serving everything from morning coffees to classic cocktails and a variety of mouth-watering dishes for lunch and dinner.

Our menus embrace the essence of our locality using the finest, locally sourced ingredients such as locally caught seafood fresh from the pier. We are passionate about food, service and delivering a dining experience that is relaxed and very much rooted in our location on the stunning Causeway Coast. So whether it's a celebratory evening with friends or a romantic table for two against the backdrop of an Atlantic sunset, we serve the best of the bay in unforgettable surroundings.
